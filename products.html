Every module shares the same tickets, staff accounts, and reporting engine — turn on what your institution needs, in the order that makes sense for you.

Core

HARDAM Queue Management

Digital ticketing with priority lanes, kiosk printing, and live counter displays.

  • Self-service kiosk & mobile ticketing
  • Priority lanes for PWD, senior citizens, pregnant clients
  • Live "now serving" displays per window
Core

HARDAM Appointment

Online booking synced to real staff calendars, with automatic reminders.

  • Public booking page per department
  • SMS & email reminders
  • No-show handling and rebooking
Add-on

HARDAM Visitor Management

Digital visitor logs, badge printing, and host notifications at the front desk.

  • ID capture and digital sign-in
  • Printed or digital visitor badges
  • Host notification on arrival
Insight

HARDAM Analytics

Wait time, throughput, and staff performance rolled up across every department.

  • Department & window-level dashboards
  • Exportable scheduled reports
  • Anomaly alerts for unusual wait times
Add-on

HARDAM POS

Point-of-sale for cashiering counters that need to bill during the same visit.

  • Fee schedules per service type
  • Receipt printing & digital receipts
  • End-of-day cash reconciliation
Core

HARDAM Student Portal

A student-facing portal for booking registrar and clinic visits from one account.
